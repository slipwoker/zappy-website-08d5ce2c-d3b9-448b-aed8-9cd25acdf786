    Our Story

    Popeat was founded with a simple yet powerful vision: to create an online marketplace to sell and discover culinary services that breaks down barriers in the food service industry. We recognized that talented private chefs often struggled to find kitchen space, restaurants had unused facility capacity during off-hours, and food entrepreneurs needed flexible solutions to grow their businesses.

    Our platform emerged from the understanding that the culinary world needed a comprehensive oracle cloud marketplace solution that could handle the unique demands of food service professionals. By leveraging modern technology and industry insights, we've built a platform that serves chefs, restaurants, catering businesses, and food enthusiasts alike.

    Today, Popeat connects thousands of culinary professionals across the industry. Our facebook marketplace api integration and advanced features make it easier than ever to find kitchen rentals, hire private chefs, list culinary services, and build profitable partnerships. We're proud to be at the forefront of transforming how the food service industry collaborates and grows.
